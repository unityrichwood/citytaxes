Do you ever wonder what your property taxes get you?

Richwood wants to give you transparent and easy-to-understand access to how your city makes your money work for you. This tool takes last year's fiscal budget and tells you where your money went, so you can see all of the value that your city provides for you. Of course, the estimates here are for informational use only and are not intended to replace the advice of a qualified tax professional.
